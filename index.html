The idea of Mike's Cereal Shack, is to have that small place in town, where you can sit down and eat your favorite kind of cereal and meet other people. We have every variety of cereal that you can buy in the store.


Michael Gary Scott is the founder of Mike's Cereal Shack and his hard-work has made this dream a reality.
"Well actually I can't cook and I am starting a restaurant. Mike's Cereal Shack. I'm thinking we'll have as many varieties as you can buy in a store" ~ Michael Scott, founder of Mike's Cereal Shack

This restaurant shows that small dreams can come true.
